Who Are We?

Established in 2020, Sisonke Solutions has swiftly emerged as a dynamic and innovative player in the Pietermaritzburg market, we specializing in comprehensive suite of services that include Printing, Signage, Clothing Supply, Marketing and Advertising. With a keen focus on delivering qaulity and customer-centric solutions. Sisonke Solutions caters to a diverse clientele, aiming to enhance their visibility and impact in the market. At the heart of Sisonke Solutions is our founder, Senzo Makhaye, who holds a majority stake, imbuing the company with his visionary leadership and enterpreneurial spirit. His passion for exellence and community partnership, with 25% ownership each by his his grandmother and mother, bringing together traditional values and mordern business acumen. This blend of generational wisdom and innovative drive makes Sisonke Solutions not just a business, but a legacy in the making. Sisonke Solutions is not merely a provider of services, it is a partner in growth for its clients. We understand the evolving needs of our market and pride ourselves on our ability to offer bespoke solutions that not only meet but exceed expectations. Our portfolio of services is designed to be a one-step solution for businesses seeking to establish or reinforce their brand presence, whether through high-qaulity printed materials, eye-catching signage, tailored clothing supplies or strategic marketing and advertising campaigns.
